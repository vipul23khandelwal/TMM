TENT MAKER MISSION for the Socity

You must not lose faith in humanity.

Missionary Bible Study Supply and Korean Children's Support Ministry

I heard that Bible books purchased in mission fields are usually expensive. We are distributing Bible books to such countries. We are working to supply biblical books and to make the Word a living. In addition, there are many people who serve mission fields with various items as well as children's hanbok. TENT MAKERS MISSION is not able to visit the mission field directly, but we also visit our mission field with the heart of our faith partners who want to serve with various goods. (In some cases, logistics costs are cheaper.)

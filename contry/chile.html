How to use air bounce playground

I want to share with you how to base your mission on the tent makers mission. It is a safe air bounce playground that gives dreams and hopes to children who do not have a playground in a poor country and gives them an opportunity to meet with the Lord.

So far we have sent over 60 bounce house playgrounds to 35 countries in 10 countries (China, Mongolia, USA, Mexico, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Philippines, Tanzania and Guatemala). Through this, many young souls were able to experience the return to Jesus. After receiving the bounce house, missionaries from around the country first lie around 2 or 4 places around the church and travel by car Where there is no church, it becomes the prime for the establishment of a church through young children.

After the children first line up, they pray together for a minute and then they start playing. I like to play praise or shake to the children and let them play with bubble balloon machines.

I will make an appointment with the children at what time next week. Prepare the children's scripture verses and share them with us. You can see all the children recite the Bible when you meet after you make an appointment. Praise and encourage children who are prepared to carry sweets or small bread and carry it on their hands will be the basis for the church building in the area If there is a missionary who needs Air bounce playground, please contact me. myungjinsik@gmail.com tentmakermission@gmail.com kakaotalk ID: us7890 USA Phone 213-503-0787

Keep your pictures and videos of children in the air bounce playground when you enter the country and use it well. It is helpful when you meet with the city hall or government official (Ministry of Education) where missionaries are staying. "We need a place for public playgrounds in the area," he said, suggesting that they provide a playground and a children's library where children can play around if they provide a place. If you get permission, we will arrange a prefabricated playground and library fixtures to be installed at our park in TENT MAKERS MISSION.
